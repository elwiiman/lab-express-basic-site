About
Fender Bass Guitars are one of the leading brands within the music industry and are commonly accredited with producing the first mainstream electric bass guitar - the Precision bass. As part of the Fender bass guitar collection, the Precision and Jazz basses are some of the most iconic. Precision basses such as the American Performer P Bass are accredited for their massive influence on music whilst Jazz basses, like the Player Jazz Bass, emit incredible high and rich tones for a fuller sound
